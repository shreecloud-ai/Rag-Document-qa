Advanced RAG Concepts
Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) combines retrieval and generation to produce more accurate LLM answers.
It uses hybrid retrieval: FAISS for semantic similarity and BM25 for keyword matching.
Reranking with cross-encoders improves the quality of top results.
Confidence scoring evaluates retrieval strength, groundedness, and chunk coverage to flag uncertain answers for human review.
Good chunking with overlap (e.g., 500 characters with 150 overlap) helps maintain context.

Benefits of RAG
RAG reduces hallucinations by grounding LLM answers in real document chunks.
It improves factual accuracy and allows the system to cite sources.
Hybrid retrieval (FAISS + BM25) + reranking gives better relevance than pure semantic or keyword search.
Confidence scoring helps flag uncertain answers for human review.
