IaaS vs SaaS
IaaS (Infrastructure as a Service) provides virtualized computing resources over the internet (servers, storage, networking).
Examples: AWS EC2, Azure Virtual Machines.
SaaS (Software as a Service) delivers software applications over the internet on a subscription basis.
Examples: Google Workspace, Microsoft Office 365, Salesforce.
Difference: IaaS gives more control and responsibility to the user, while SaaS is fully managed by the provider.
