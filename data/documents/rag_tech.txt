RAG Technology
Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) is a technique that retrieves relevant document chunks before generating an answer with an LLM.
It reduces hallucinations and grounds the response in real data.
Key components: chunking, Sentence-BERT embeddings, FAISS vector search, BM25 keyword search, reranking, and confidence scoring.
Hybrid retrieval (FAISS + BM25) usually performs better than using either alone.
